🍝 Classic Pasta 🍝

Ingredients 🍲

  • 200g Pasta
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp Butter

Recipe 🍽

  • 200g pasta ko namak wale pani me al dente tak ubal lo.
  • Ek pan me butter melt karo aur garlic ko sauté karo jab tak khushboo aaye.
  • Heavy cream aur Parmesan cheese mila do.
  • Salt aur black pepper se taste adjust karo.
  • Pasta ko creamy sauce me toss karo.
  • Zarurat ho to thoda pasta water add karo sauce ko smooth banane ke liye.
  • Parsley garnish karke garam-garam serve karo.
  • Ye dish rich, cheesy, aur 350–400 calories ke aas-paas hai.

🥗 Fresh Salad 🥗

Ingredients 🥦

  • Mixed Greens
  • Cucumber, Carrots, Tomatoes
  • Avocado, Boiled Egg
  • Feta Cheese, Sunflower Seeds

Recipe 🍽

  • Mixed greens, sliced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, shredded carrots, aur thinly sliced red onion ko ek bowl me daalo.
  • Sliced avocado aur ek boiled egg add karo protein ke liye.
  • Upar se feta cheese crumble karo.
  • Sunflower seeds sprinkle karo crunch ke liye.
  • Olive oil aur balsamic vinegar drizzle karo.
  • Dheere se toss karke mix karo.
  • Ye simple aur fresh salad flavorful hai aur ek perfect light meal hai.

🎂 Chocolate Cake 🎂

Ingredients 🧁

  • Flour, Cocoa Powder, Sugar
  • Baking Powder & Soda
  • Eggs, Milk, Oil

Recipe 🍽

  • Oven ko 350°F (175°C) par preheat karo aur 9-inch round cake pan ko grease aur flour karo.
  • Ek large bowl me flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, aur salt sift karke mix karo.
  • Eggs, milk, vegetable oil, aur vanilla extract add karo aur smooth hone tak mix karo.
  • Dheere-dheere boiling water add karo (batter thoda patla hoga).
  • Batter ko prepared pan me daal do aur 30–35 minutes bake karo ya jab tak toothpick clean na nikle.
  • Cake ko pan me 10 minutes ke liye thanda hone do, phir wire rack par completely cool hone ke liye rakho.
  • Cool hone ke baad chaaho to frosting karo aur enjoy karo!
